About CodeRise

Empowering beginners to rise in tech by teaching coding skills that matter.

Our Mission

At CodeRise by Miami FX, we believe coding should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to guide beginners through the journey of learning web development step by step — from building simple websites to creating full-stack applications.

Our Vision

We envision a future where anyone with curiosity can unlock opportunities in tech. By offering an easy-to-follow learning model (via WhatsApp and structured lessons), we make coding less intimidating and more engaging.

Why Choose CodeRise?

Beginner-Friendly

Learn with guidance, discussions, and tasks directly on WhatsApp.

Step-by-Step Growth

We help you build projects and grow your skills progressively.
